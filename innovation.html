Invention & Innovation

Creation and implementation of a novel System Design Verification Test methodology for Enphase Energy's first EV charger platform, validating the complete ecosystem across hardware, firmware, cloud, mobile applications, energy systems, and vehicle interoperability.

Professional Narrative

The innovation transformed verification from sequential subsystem testing into a unified system-level validation model.

Core Innovation

Novel SDVT methodology validating end-to-end behavior across component boundaries and reducing validation cycles from months to hours.

Methodology

Authored requirements, architecture, and SDVT plans with traceability across requirements, verification activities, and outcomes.

External Validation

Represented Enphase at CharIN Testival, an EV interoperability event involving vehicle and charging ecosystem stakeholders.
